Q: Why should I use this document translation app?
A: Our app provides fast, accurate, and affordable translation services for various document types, helping you communicate effectively across languages.
Q: How do I upload documents for translation?
A: Simply download our app, sign in, and use the "Upload" feature to select your documents for translation.
Q: What languages do you support?
A: We support a wide range of languages, including but not limited to English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, and Arabic. Check our website for a full list.
Q: How much does it cost to translate a document?
A: The cost depends on the document length and the language pair. You can view estimated prices within the app before finalizing your order.
Q: How does the translation process work?
A: Our app uses advanced algorithms and experienced translators to ensure high-quality translations. Once you submit a document, it is processed and translated by our system or a professional translator.
Q: How do I receive my translated document?
A: After your document is translated, it will be available for download directly from the app or sent to your registered email.
Q: What if I need to make changes to the translation?
A: You can request revisions through the app, and our team will assist you with any necessary adjustments.
Q: Can I use the app for business documents?
A: Yes, our app is designed to handle both personal and business documents with confidentiality and professionalism.
Q: How secure is my data?
A: We prioritize your privacy and security. All documents are encrypted during transmission and storage, and we do not share your data with third parties.
Q: What if I have a complaint about my translation?
A: We value customer feedback. If you're unsatisfied with your translation, please contact our support team via the app for assistance.
Q: Can I track the status of my translation?
A: Yes, you can easily track the progress of your translation within the app, and you'll receive notifications when it's complete.
Q: How do I contact customer support?
A: Our customer support team is available 24/7 through the app or via email for any inquiries or assistance you may need.
Q: Is there a limit on the number of documents I can translate?
A: There is no strict limit. However, large volumes may take longer to process. You can manage your uploads through the app.
Q: Can I request specific formatting for my translated document?
A: Yes, you can specify your formatting preferences when uploading your document, and our team will do their best to accommodate your requests.
Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept various payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal. You can select your preferred method during checkout.
